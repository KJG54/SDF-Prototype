# Phase 2: Requirements

## Purpose

Turn the vision into an approved project contract.

## Agent Responsibilities

- Create `REQ-001`.
- Clarify must-have, nice-to-have, out-of-scope, constraints, assumptions, and success criteria.
- Resolve or explicitly document core open questions.
- Update `PROJECT-CHECKLIST.md` and `project-checklist.json`.
- Ask approval before moving on.

## Human Responsibilities

- Confirm what belongs in v1.
- Approve or revise requirements.

## Required Output

- `REQ-001-requirements.json`
- `REQ-001-requirements.md`
- Phase gate
- Phase summary

## Exit Criteria

The human approves requirements or asks for revision.
